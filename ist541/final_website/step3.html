Step 3: Direct Cutting

Instructions from Roland DG Corporation GS-24 User's Manual



Step 3.1: Open "Cutting Setup"

If not yet open, open the 'Cutting Setup' window in CutStudio by going to 'File' and selecting 'Cutting Setup' from the menu that appears.


Image from GS-24 User's Manual



Step 3.2: Select "Change"

In the open 'Cutting Setup' window, select 'Change' under 'Media Size.'


Image from "Cut Your Sticker" video



Step 3.3: Select "Get from Machine"

In the 'Roland GS-24 Properties' window that opens, select 'Get from Machine.' This will allow the vinyl cutter to analyze the material.


Image from "Cut Your Sticker" video



Step 3.4: Select "OK"

Select 'OK' in the 'Roland GS-24 Properties' window.


Image from GS-24 User's Manual



Step 3.5: Select "OK" Again

Select 'OK' in the 'Cutting Setup' window.


Image from GS-24 User's Manual



Step 3.6: Select "Cutting"

In CutStudio (where you will have sent your prepared design data) select "Cutting" from the top menu bar.


Image from GS-24 User's Manual



Step 3.7: Select "OK"

After you select "Cutting" a window will open.

To direct the vinyl cutter to start cutting your design, select "OK" in the bottom right corner.

error_outline Stay clear of the cutting area after you select "OK," as the blade will be in motion.

You're ready for Step 4: Remove Material

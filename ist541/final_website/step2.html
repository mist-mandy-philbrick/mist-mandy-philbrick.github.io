Step 2: Set Up the Vinyl Cutter

Instructions from Roland DG Corporation GS-24 User's Manual



Step 2.1: Safety First!

Be careful not to put your fingers in the cutting area when the vinyl cutter is on.

error_outline The cutting carriage, which holds a sharp blade, is designed to move across the cutting area.


Image taken by Mandy Harris in the Innovation Lab



Step 2.2: Connect to Power and Computer

Ensure the power cord is plugged into an outlet.

Connect the vinyl cutter to the computer with your prepared design data with a USB cord. This will allow design data to be shared with the vinyl cutter.

Image from GS-24 User's Manual



Step 2.3: Press the Power Button Once

The power button will turn blue.

Image taken by Mandy Harris in the Innovation Lab



Step 2.4: Display either 'ROLL' or 'PIECE'

Select either the 'up' or 'down' cursor until 'PIECE' appears in the display.

Note: For current use of the Innovation Lab vinyl cutter, select 'ROLL' even when using pieces of vinyl.



Images from GS-24 User's Manual



Step 2.5: Press 'ENTER'

The cutting carriage will move to where you placed the left pinch roller. It may also move backward and forward.

Image from GS-24 User's Manual



Step 2.6: Press the 'MENU' button

The settings will appear in the display

You're ready for Step 3: Direct Cutting

Image from GS-24 User's Manual

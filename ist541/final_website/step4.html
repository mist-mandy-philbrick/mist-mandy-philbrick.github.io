Step 4: Remove Material

Instructions from Roland DG Corporation GS-24 User's Manual



Step 4.1: Display 'UNSETUP'

Select either the 'up' or 'down' cursor until 'PIECE' appears in the display.


Image from GS-24 User's Manual



Step 4.2: Select 'ENTER'

Once 'UNSETUP' appears in the display, select the 'ENTER' button.

The cutting carriage will return to its home position by the operation panel.

Image from GS-24 User's Manual



Step 4.3: Lower the Loading Lever and Remove Material

Once the cutting carriage is home by the operation panel, you're ready to unclamp your material.

Gently lower the loading lever to release your cut design.

Remove the material through either the front or back of the machine.

You're ready for the last step: Step 5: Turn Machine Off

Image from GS-24 User's Manual
